Welcome to Singapore - The land of the Lionmaid. Most of you are already Singaporean so not much I can say here. It's hot. It's humid. It's expensive. But it's great!

The entire ordeal will happen at the Pollen. So prepare lots of antihistamines ha ha ha!

We'd love to see everyone, but due to limited spaces we cannot. So the day is getting split into two parts:

1) Breakfast will be held between 10:00-11:30 including a tea cervix, early drinking and croissants and some general mangling around.

This part is open to anyone who has landed on this page. If you are coming just to this part - we highly encourage you to come as early as possible since we'll have to start prepping for the rest pretty soon after.

2) Once over, a traditional chinese tea ceremony is to be held at about 11:30 for adults and family and whatnot, after which we'll transition smoothly to a 18 course lunch. The lunch will finish at around 15:00.

Since you have an entry to the Pollen, you'll automatically be able to browse around the greenhouse afterwords for no additional fee, so do enjoy!

Also there'll be a buggy service from the main entrance of Gardens by the Bay that'll fetch you straight to the Pollen. Further details will be provided.

And after all that a selected few (close family and special friends) shall be moving our asses over to Bintan, Indonesia for a couple of nights. Nice.

Hope you'll all enjoy the mega feast and gain lots of weight.

See you all there!
